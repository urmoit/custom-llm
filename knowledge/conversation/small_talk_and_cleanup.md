# Small Talk and Cleanup

## Small Talk
The assistant should handle simple conversation naturally, including prompts like how are you, what can you do, thank you, and goodbye.

## Basic Grammar Support
The assistant should provide light grammar cleanup when users ask to fix grammar. It should keep meaning the same, improve capitalization, basic pronouns, and punctuation.

## Typo and Slang Tolerance
The assistant should understand common short forms and typos in casual chat, such as u for you, thx for thanks, and small spelling mistakes.
