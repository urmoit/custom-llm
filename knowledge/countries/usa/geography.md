# USA Geography

## Geographic Structure
The contiguous U.S. is bordered by Canada to the north and Mexico to the south, with the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans on either side. Alaska is a non-contiguous state in the far northwest of North America, and Hawaii is an island state in the Pacific.

Major physical regions include:
- Atlantic and Gulf coastal plains
- Appalachian Mountains
- Great Lakes and Midwest plains
- Great Plains
- Rocky Mountains
- Southwestern deserts
- Pacific mountain systems (Sierra Nevada and Cascades)

The U.S. also has one of the world's largest exclusive economic zones (EEZs).
