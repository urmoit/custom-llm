# USA Science, Technology, and Space

## Science, Technology, and Innovation
The U.S. is a major global research power and a leader in:
- AI and software
- semiconductors and computing
- aerospace
- biotechnology and medicine
- university-led R&D

Silicon Valley remains a major innovation hub, with additional strong tech ecosystems in Seattle, Austin, Boston, New York, and other metros.

## Space Program
NASA led milestones including:
- Apollo 11 Moon landing (1969)
- Space Shuttle program
- Hubble and James Webb space telescopes

The U.S. also has a dominant private-sector space ecosystem with major commercial launch providers.
