# USA Government and Politics

## Government and Constitutional Order
The U.S. is a constitutional federal republic with separation of powers.

Federal branches:
- Legislative: Congress (Senate + House of Representatives)
- Executive: President and federal agencies
- Judicial: Supreme Court and federal courts

Federalism gives substantial powers to state governments. The U.S. also recognizes tribal sovereignty for federally recognized Native American tribes.

## Political System
The two largest national parties are:
- Democratic Party
- Republican Party

The U.S. operates as a de facto two-party system, with additional state and local parties existing in smaller roles.
