# USA Sports, Cities, Climate, and Policy

## Sports
Major sports include:
- American football (NFL)
- basketball (NBA)
- baseball (MLB)
- ice hockey (NHL)
- soccer (MLS)

College sports also play a major role in U.S. culture and talent development.

## Cities and Metropolitan Areas
Large metro areas include New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Dallas-Fort Worth, Houston, Washington, Miami, Philadelphia, Phoenix, Boston, and others.

## Climate and Biodiversity
The U.S. spans many climate zones: tropical, arid, Mediterranean, temperate, continental, subarctic, and polar (in Alaska). It is a megadiverse country with extensive protected lands, national parks, and varied ecosystems.

## Ongoing Policy Challenges
Common recurring policy debates include:
- healthcare affordability and coverage
- housing costs and homelessness
- income and wealth inequality
- immigration reform
- climate resilience and energy transition
- political polarization and democratic governance
- education access and student debt

## Practical Snapshot
Daily life varies significantly by state and metro. Cost of living, transit access, taxation, and public services differ widely by location. This regional variation is a defining feature of the U.S. experience.
