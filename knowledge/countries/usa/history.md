# USA History

## Historical Arc (High-Level)
- Indigenous civilizations existed long before European colonization.
- 1776: Declaration of Independence.
- 1787 to 1789: Constitution created and implemented.
- 1861 to 1865: Civil War; slavery abolished afterward.
- 20th century: industrial and geopolitical rise; major role in both world wars.
- Cold War era: superpower rivalry with the Soviet Union.
- Post-1991: major global influence in economics, military affairs, technology, and media.
