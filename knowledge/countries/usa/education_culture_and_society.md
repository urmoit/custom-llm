# USA Education, Culture, and Society

## Education and Research Institutions
The U.S. has a large higher education system with globally recognized universities, strong public university networks, and significant private research institutions. It is a top destination for international students and research output.

## Culture and Society
U.S. culture is highly diverse and globally influential in:
- film and television
- music
- internet and digital media
- fashion
- sports

American culture combines long-standing local traditions with strong influence from immigrant communities.

## Religion and Belief Landscape
The U.S. has broad religious diversity with Christianity as the largest affiliation, alongside Judaism, Islam, Buddhism, Hinduism, and non-religious populations. Religious freedom is constitutionally protected.
