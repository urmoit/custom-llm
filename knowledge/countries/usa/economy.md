# USA Economy

## Economy
The U.S. has the world's largest economy by nominal GDP. It is highly diversified across:
- services and finance
- technology and software
- manufacturing
- healthcare and biotech
- energy
- agriculture
- aerospace and defense

Key economic centers include New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Dallas-Fort Worth, Houston, San Francisco Bay Area, Seattle, Boston, and Washington metro.

The U.S. dollar is the leading global reserve and transaction currency.
