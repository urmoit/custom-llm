# USA Identity and Core Facts

## Identity and Core Facts
The United States of America is a federal republic primarily located in North America. It consists of 50 states, Washington, D.C. (federal capital district), and five major inhabited territories. The country is among the world's largest by total area and has one of the largest populations globally.

Capital: Washington, D.C.
Largest city: New York City
Currency: U.S. dollar (USD)
Internet domains: `.com`, `.us`

## States, District, Territories, and Tribal Sovereignty
- 50 states
- 1 federal district (Washington, D.C.)
- 5 major inhabited territories: Puerto Rico, Guam, U.S. Virgin Islands, American Samoa, Northern Mariana Islands
- Hundreds of federally recognized tribes and reservations
