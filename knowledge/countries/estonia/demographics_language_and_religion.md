# Estonia Demographics, Language, and Religion

## Demographics
Estonia has a population of about 1.3 to 1.4 million people. Most residents live in urban areas, especially around Tallinn and Tartu.

## Ethnic Composition
Ethnic Estonians are the majority, with a significant Russian-speaking minority and smaller Ukrainian and other communities.

## Language
Estonian is the official language and the main first language in the country. Russian is still widely understood in some communities, and English is common among younger people.

## Religion
Estonia is one of the least religious countries in the world. Most people identify as non-religious, with Christianity and Eastern Orthodoxy forming the largest religious groups among believers.
