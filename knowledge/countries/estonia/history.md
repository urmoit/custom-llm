# Estonia History

## Early History
People have lived in the area for thousands of years. The region developed distinct Finnic culture, trade links, and local county structures before the medieval crusades.

## Foreign Rule
Estonia was ruled at different times by Danish, German, Swedish, and Russian powers. The medieval and early modern periods strongly shaped its institutions, language, and towns.

## National Awakening
In the 19th century, Estonian identity strengthened through language, education, literature, and song festivals.

## Modern Independence
- 1918: first declaration of independence
- 1940 to 1991: Soviet occupation with wartime German occupation in between
- 1988 to 1991: Singing Revolution and restored independence

## Contemporary Era
After independence, Estonia moved quickly toward market reform, digital government, and Western integration.
