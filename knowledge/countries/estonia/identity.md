# Estonia Identity and Core Facts

## Identity and Core Facts
Estonia is a small Baltic country in Northern Europe. It is a democratic unitary parliamentary republic with Tallinn as its capital and largest city.

Official language: Estonian
Currency: Euro (€)
Internet domain: `.ee`
Time zone: EET, with EEST in summer

## State Continuity and Independence
- Independent republic first declared on 24 February 1918
- Occupied during World War II and the Soviet period
- Restored independence on 20 August 1991

## Membership and Alignment
- Member of the European Union
- Member of NATO
- Member of the eurozone
- Member of the Schengen Area
