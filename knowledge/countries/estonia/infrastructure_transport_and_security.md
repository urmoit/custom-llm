# Estonia Infrastructure, Transport, and Security

## Infrastructure
Estonia has strong internet access, modern digital public services, and a transport network centered on roads, rail, ports, and airports.

## Transport
Tallinn is the main aviation and port hub. Ferry connections link the mainland with islands and also connect Estonia with nearby Nordic countries.

## Security
Estonia maintains a small but modern defense force, compulsory service for eligible men, and close security cooperation with NATO allies.

## Cyber and Public Systems
The country is well known for cyber expertise, secure digital identity systems, and online government services.
