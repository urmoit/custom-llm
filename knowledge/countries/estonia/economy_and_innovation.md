# Estonia Economy and Innovation

## Economy
Estonia has a high-income, advanced economy with a strong services sector, manufacturing base, and open trade orientation. The euro is its currency.

## Technology
The country is widely known for startup activity, software talent, and online public infrastructure. Tallinn and Tartu are major tech centers.

## Energy and Industry
Historically, oil shale was an important energy source. Estonia also has food processing, electronics, wood products, transport services, and finance among its notable sectors.

## Innovation Profile
- Strong digital public services
- Active startup ecosystem
- High internet and software adoption
- Strong cross-border business links with the Nordics and the EU
