# Estonia Geography and Climate

## Geography
Estonia sits on the eastern shore of the Baltic Sea. It borders Latvia to the south and Russia to the east, and it lies across the Gulf of Finland from Finland.

The country includes the mainland plus many islands, including Saaremaa and Hiiumaa. Its terrain is mostly flat, with forests, bogs, lakes, and a long coastline.

## Climate
Estonia has a cool transitional climate shaped by both continental and maritime influences. Winters are cold and dark, while summers are mild.

## Natural Features
- Many islands and islets
- Large forest cover
- Numerous lakes, rivers, and wetlands
- Strong seasonal daylight variation
