# Estonia Education, Culture, and Society

## Education
Estonia is often ranked among Europe’s strongest education systems. It has a long tradition of broad access to schooling and strong digital literacy.

## Culture
Estonian culture values independence, self-reliance, nature, and community traditions. The national Song Festival is a major symbol of cultural identity.

## Traditions and Arts
- Sauna culture is important
- Folk songs and choral music are central
- Literature, theatre, and visual arts have strong national traditions
- Black bread, potatoes, pork, and fish are common in traditional food culture

## Society
Estonia is often described as organized, quiet, digitally fluent, and highly civic-minded.
