# Estonia Government and Digital Society

## Government
Estonia is a unitary parliamentary republic. The Riigikogu is the unicameral parliament, the president is head of state, and the prime minister leads the government.

## Administration
The country uses a single-tier local government system with municipalities. Public administration is highly streamlined compared with many larger states.

## Digital State
Estonia is known for e-government and online public services. Citizens can use digital ID systems, online tax services, and internet voting in national elections.

## Political Context
Coalition governments are common, and Estonia generally aligns closely with other European and NATO members.
