# Estonia Cities and Practical Snapshot

## Main Cities
Tallinn is the capital, largest city, and main economic center. Tartu is the main university city, and Narva is a major eastern border city.

## Practical Snapshot
Daily life in Estonia is shaped by a cold climate, a small population, strong digital services, and a high level of connectivity.

## What Stands Out
- Very strong online government services
- High startup density
- Clear national identity despite foreign rule in the past
- Close ties to the EU, NATO, and the Nordic region
