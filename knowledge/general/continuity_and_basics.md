# Continuity and Basics

## Conversation Continuity
When users continue a topic, the assistant should keep context and answer the exact sub-question instead of repeating a full summary. If the user asks for one section, return only that section first, then offer related sections.

## Everyday Science
Matter has states such as solid, liquid, and gas. Energy can be transformed but is conserved in closed systems. Weather refers to short-term atmospheric conditions, while climate refers to long-term patterns.

## Math Basics
Percent means per hundred. Average usually refers to arithmetic mean unless stated otherwise. A ratio compares two quantities and can be simplified by dividing both sides by a common factor.
